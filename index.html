Violin, Viola & Piano Lessons

Elizabeth Chirgwin M.M.

Artistic Director

Elizabeth Chirgwin maintains an active career as a performer and educator. A Masters graduate in violin performance from New York University, Ms. Chirgwin previously trained at Jacobs School of Music at Indiana University in Bloomington, and Chicago College of Performing Arts, Roosevelt University. After receiving her Bachelors and Masters in Music, Ms. Chirgwin performed with The California Symphony, The Fremont Symphony as assistant principal second, Santa Cruz Symphony, Wheeling Symphony Orchestra, Charleston Symphony Orchestra, Erie Philharmonic, Indiana University Festival Orchestra, among others. Ms. Chirgwin has had the privilege to perform under music directors Keith Lockhart, David Effron, Robert Spano, Lawrence Renes and Xian Zhang. Her teachers and orchestral coaches include: Kurt Loebel, Alex Kerr, William Preucil, Yuko Mori, and Greg Fulkerson. Ms. Chirgwin received scholarships for solo and chamber performances at Killington Music Festival, Stony Brook Summer Music Festival, Aria International music festival, Indiana University Summer Music Festival and Brevard Music Festival. As the founder and an educator of Contra Costa School of Music, Ms. Chirgwin has been on faculty with The Winthrop School of Performing Arts, Young Artist Conservatory of Music, Musika, and Akron University preparatory department. Recently, she joined faculty at Walnut Creek Civic Arts Education.
